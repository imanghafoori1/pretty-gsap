Write this:
      animate( logo ).during( 2 ).to( {left:"440px"} ).easeOut( Back ).start();


Instead of this :
      TweenLite.to(logo, 2, { left:"440px", ease:Back.easeOut } );



.easeInOut()
.easeIn()
are also available.

For some cases that none are the above applicable you can write:
      animate( logo ).during( 2 ).to( {left:"440px"} ).ease( SlowMo, 'ease' ).start();
