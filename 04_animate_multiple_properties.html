Write this:
      animate( logo ).during( 2 ).to( {left:"542px", backgroundColor:"black", borderBottomColor:"#90e500", color:"white"} ).startNow();


Instead of this :
      TweenLite.to(logo, 2, {left:"542px", backgroundColor:"black", borderBottomColor:"#90e500", color:"white"});


Anything you passed as 3th parameter to the Tweenlite.from() (or .to) method, you now can pass to .from() (or .to)
